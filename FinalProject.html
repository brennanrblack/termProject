Career

  

In five years, I hope to have made progress towards building a career that fulfills me. I am currently interested in working in either a sales or marketing role for a software company. I am interested in working in tech because of the impact these company's products have on society and because of the workplace culture at these firms. I want to work for a company that is doing something new and exciting while also providing a benefit to society. The notion of social benefit is espcecially important to me because I don't just want to have a job to make money; I to love what I or at the very least feel that what I do is important and helps others. Personal Life

  

I hope that by the age of 26 I have developed strong self discipline. At this current point in my life, I feel that I am held back from achieving my goals because I am not disciplined. I want to have a more normal sleep schedule so that I can fell well rested and ready for the day. I want to make fitness a priority every day so that I can be healthy and have the physical and mental strength to take on the challenges that are ahead of me. I want to eat clean so that I can be energized throughout the day and take care of my body so that I can live a long and healthy life. These things are easy in concept but very difficult in practice. It takes a lot of hard work to break bad habits and to develop the internalized discipline to go against your base desires. This is something that I have struggled with for a while but I am determined to make it happen because I know that it will improve the quality of my life and will make me a more productive and positive person.
